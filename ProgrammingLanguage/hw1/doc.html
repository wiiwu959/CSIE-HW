Account.java

  • 在不同的Account class裡面按照題目要求實作deposit、withdraw、compute_interest三種method，以及根據account性質不同在construct時有些微不同。
  • 在SavingAccount和CDAccount的月份處理上，並非使用30天計算，而是以Calendar來處理Date中的MONTH、YEAR來算。
  • 計算利息仍然用30天為一個月來計算
  • Classes
    • BasicAccount
    • BankingException
    • CDAccount
    • CheckingAccount
    • DepositableAccount
    • FullFunctionalAccout
    • InterestableAccount
    • LoanAccount
    • SavingAccount
    • WithdrawableAccount

CheckingAccount

  • requirement: interest is computed daily; there’s no fee for withdrawals; there is a minimum balance of $1000.
  • 使用原本給的範例原樣

SavingAccount

  • requirement: monthly interest; fee of $1 for every transaction, except the first three per month are free; no minimum balance.
  • 使用 transactionTimes 和 Date lastTransactionDate 來計算並記錄當月的transaction次數，當月超過三次就要收取fee $1

CDAccount

  • requirement: monthly interest; fixed amount and duration (e.g., you can open 1 12-month CD for $5000; for the next 12 months you can’t deposit anything and withdrawals cost a $250 fee); at the end of the duration the interest payments stop and you can withdraw w/o fee.
  • 用 duration 來紀錄從存入到當前經過的月份數，判斷能不能存錢和提款是否需要收fee $250
  • 在construct account時即指定要限制的月份數，若沒有指定default為12個月

LoanAccount

  • requirement: like a saving account, but the balance is “negative” (you owe the bank money, so a deposit will reduce the amount of the loan); you can’t withdraw (i.e., loan more money) but of course you can deposit (i.e., pay off part of the loan).
  • 帳戶只能還錢不能提款
  • 利息以月計算

Application.java

  • 造出四種不同account
  • 根據不同account特性，測試出特點

CheckingAccount

  • 存款、提款
  • 提款數超過用戶財產(exception)
  • 計算一月個後利息

SavingAccount

  • 當月三次內transaction之存款、提款
  • 超過當月三次transaction之提款(收fee $1)
  • 計算兩個月後利息

CDAccount

  • 在限制時間內存款(無法)、提款(收fee $250)
  • 在限制時間結束後存款、提款
  • 計算十三個月後利息

LoanAccount

  • 存款、提款(無法)
  • 計算一個月後利息

Inheritance hierarchy

  • BasicAccount
    • WithdrawableAccount
      • CDAccount
    • DepositableAccount
      • LoanAccount
    • InterestableAccount
      • SavingAccount
    • FullFunctionalAccount
      • extends WithdrawableAccount、DepositableAccount、InterestableAccount
      • CheckingAccount
